# Pet Dolphin - Flipper Zero Documentation

## Introduction

The Flipper Zero contains a digital dolphin companion that engages with access control technologies including Sub-GHz radio, RFID, NFC, infrared, 1-Wire, and BadUSB. Regular device usage makes the digital pet happy and allows you to witness its emotional evolution.

## Meeting Your Digital Pet

The desktop serves as your pet's home where it works, learns, and rests. You can observe its activities and emotions through various animations.

**Accessing the Passport:**
- Press the right button while on the desktop, or
- Navigate to Main Menu > Settings > Passport

The passport displays the pet's appearance, unique name, emotional state, level, and progress bar.

## Keeping Your Pet Happy

Your dolphin's mood depends on how often you interact with the Flipper Zero. To maintain happiness:
- Use Flipper Zero applications regularly
- Interact with access control systems by reading, saving, and emulating keys, cards, and remotes
- Enable Happy Mode in settings if you want to prevent the pet from becoming sad or angry

## Leveling Up Your Dolphin

Your digital pet progresses through three levels. As it advances, it transforms its appearance and develops new interests.

**Level Progression Requirements:**
- Level 2: 300 XP
- Level 3: 1,800 XP

**Daily XP Caps:**
- Maximum 20 XP per application per day
- Maximum 140 XP overall per day
