# HID Controllers - Flipper Zero Documentation

## Overview

The Remote app enables your Flipper Zero to function as a Human Interface Device (HID) controller for computers and phones. It can control presentations, emulate input devices, manage media playback, and more.

## Pairing Your Flipper Zero

### Via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)

1. Enable Bluetooth on both your computer/phone and Flipper Zero
2. On Flipper Zero: navigate to Main Menu > Settings > Bluetooth and turn it on
3. Go to Main Menu > Apps > Bluetooth > Remote
4. On your computer or phone, open Bluetooth settings and select "Control [device name]"
5. On Flipper Zero, press OK to complete pairing

**Troubleshooting:**
- Verify Bluetooth is enabled on both devices
- Check connection status on your computer/phone
- Reboot by holding Left and Back buttons for 5 seconds
- Forget all paired devices and reconnect
- Update to the latest firmware

### Via USB Cable

Connect your Flipper Zero to your computer with a USB-C cable, then navigate to Main Menu > Apps > USB > Remote.

## Supported Applications

**Keynote** - Present slideshows using directional buttons to navigate slides

**Keyboard** - Type text by selecting characters; access symbols and modifier keys (Ctrl, Alt, Cmd)

**Media** - Control playback, skip tracks, and adjust volume on media players

**Camera Remote** - Shoot photos or record videos on connected phones

**Mouse** - Move cursor and perform left/right clicks using directional buttons

**TikTok Controller** - Navigate videos, like content, and adjust volume

**Mouse Jiggler & Mouse Clicker** - Prevent computers from entering sleep mode by simulating mouse activity
