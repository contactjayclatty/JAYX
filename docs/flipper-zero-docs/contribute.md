# Contributing to Flipper Docs

## How to Suggest Changes

To contribute to Flipper Docs, follow these steps:

1. **Locate the suggest changes button** - On the page you want to contribute to, click the "Suggest Changes" button below the table of contents

2. **Enter your changes** - In the open editor, input the modifications you'd like to suggest

3. **Add formatted content** - To insert headers, tables, images, or other blocks, type "/" and select the desired block type. For available blocks and descriptions, consult the editor guide at archbee.com/docs/editor-blocks

4. **Submit for review** - Once finished, enter your email and provide a brief description of your changes at the bottom of the editor window, then click "Send for Review"

5. **Confirm your suggestion** - You'll receive a confirmation email—click the link to verify your suggestion

6. **Await approval** - The team will review your suggestions and merge approved changes into Flipper Docs

## Questions?

The support team and community are available to help. Submit questions in English through the authentication-required forum.
